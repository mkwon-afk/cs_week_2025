**QUESTION 1:**
According to the Future of Jobs Report 2025, what percentage of jobs are expected to be disrupted by AI and automation?
A) 25%
B) 40%
C) 60%
D) 75%

**Correct Answer:** B) 40%
**Explanation:** The Future of Jobs Report 2025 indicates that approximately 40% of jobs will be disrupted by AI and automation, requiring significant reskilling and adaptation.

---

**QUESTION 2:**
Which skill is predicted to be most in-demand for customer service roles by 2025?
A) Data Analysis
B) Emotional Intelligence
C) Coding
D) Project Management

**Correct Answer:** B) Emotional Intelligence
**Explanation:** Emotional Intelligence is increasingly crucial as AI handles routine tasks, leaving humans to focus on complex emotional interactions and relationship building.

---

**QUESTION 3:**
What is the expected growth rate for 'green jobs' in the renewable energy sector by 2025?
A) 15%
B) 25%
C) 35%
D) 50%

**Correct Answer:** C) 35%
**Explanation:** Green jobs in renewable energy are expected to grow by approximately 35% by 2025, reflecting the global shift toward sustainability.

---

**QUESTION 4:**
Which technology is predicted to create the most new jobs by 2025?
A) Artificial Intelligence
B) Blockchain
C) Quantum Computing
D) Augmented Reality

**Correct Answer:** A) Artificial Intelligence
**Explanation:** AI is expected to create more jobs than it displaces, particularly in AI training, oversight, and human-AI collaboration roles.

---

**QUESTION 5:**
What percentage of workers will need to reskill by 2025 according to the report?
A) 30%
B) 50%
C) 70%
D) 90%

**Correct Answer:** B) 50%
**Explanation:** Approximately 50% of workers will need to reskill by 2025 to adapt to changing job requirements and new technologies.

---

**QUESTION 6:**
Which industry is expected to see the highest job growth in customer-facing roles?
A) Healthcare
B) Education
C) Retail
D) Financial Services

**Correct Answer:** A) Healthcare
**Explanation:** Healthcare is expected to see the highest growth in customer-facing roles due to aging populations and increased demand for personalized care.

---

**QUESTION 7:**
What is the predicted impact of remote work on job opportunities by 2025?
A) Decrease by 20%
B) Stay the same
C) Increase by 30%
D) Increase by 50%

**Correct Answer:** C) Increase by 30%
**Explanation:** Remote work opportunities are expected to increase by approximately 30% by 2025, expanding access to global talent pools.

---

**QUESTION 8:**
Which soft skill is becoming increasingly important for future customer service success?
A) Critical Thinking
B) Creativity
C) Adaptability
D) All of the above

**Correct Answer:** D) All of the above
**Explanation:** All three skills - Critical Thinking, Creativity, and Adaptability - are becoming increasingly important as automation handles routine tasks.
